What can you do to avoid impending doom?

Just because the chances are slim that we can survive on this planet doesn't mean we should just give up on it!

Building a global economy capable of supporting interstellar travel and terraforming on a planatery scale is going to take alot of work.

There are multiple things you, as a consumer and a human being, can do to try to give us as much time as we can to get the hell out of dodge.

Recycle and Reuse

You've seen the movie Wall-E right? well we're heading in that direction, and with a population that keeps growing exponentially, it won't be long.

Incase you haven't seen it

Invest In Renewable Energy

Elon Musk's Tesla is doing great things in that departement. I mean he just unveiled roofing tiles that act as solar panels that are cheaper and longer lasting then a regular roof... what are you waiting for?

Seriously, what are you waiting for?

Money is Power!

People tend to forget how much power we have as consumers. In a modern world people don't vote with their ballot. They vote with their wallet. Spend your money on products and companies that have the idea of a sustainable future at heart.

Vote!

New Forms Of Energy

All this talk of solar power is all well and good, but outside of building a dyson sphere around the sun it will only take us so far. Are you an engineer? a physicist? an eccentric billionaire? Then work the problem! The film Moon had an interesting idea involving helium-3 and cold fusion.

It's a pretty good movie

Mars?

It's close(ish), we're pretty sure it used to sustain life and an aforementioned man/corporation is planning on getting us there in just a few short years. Colonizing mars is now a real possibility. let's freaking do it!

SpaceX

Overpopulation

Overpopulation is always the elephant in the room. Everyone admits it's a problem but very rarely do we delve into how MUCH of a problem it is. If our birthrates stay unchecked it will be the death of us. Climate change or not, we will run out of space and food eventually. So for now maybe come to grips with the realisation that we don't need to make babies anymore, maybe put a condom on. Maybe, on a cloudless night, look up and realise that our future is out there.
