What Comes Next?

Climate Change... Given enough time it becomes inevitable. sprinkle in a few billion hungry, greedy humans and we have a recipe for disaster. Our species has outgrown this beautiful planet of ours. We can change and dress the wound as much as we want but the damage is done. So what the hell do we do now?

We at Next Step believe that, even though the outlook looks bleak, it does not spell the end for our species. It is mearly the end as we know it. We are a group of like minded individuals that have made it our sole purpose to bring awareness to the masses about the steps we have to take to bring us closer to our destiny. The Stars
